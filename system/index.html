Elevate Your Daily Routine with "Three Daily Goals" – The Essential Daily Review Process

Experience a structured and efficient way to manage your tasks with "Three Daily Goals," your personal productivity coach. Our app's daily triggered process ensures you start and end each day with clarity and control. Here's a breakdown of the daily review process in markdown format:

Daily Triggered Review Process

Every day, at a time you've set, "Three Daily Goals" activates the daily review process. This system is designed to help you reflect, organise, and prioritise with precision. Embark on this transformative journey daily to maintain momentum and achieve your goals.

1. Review & Close Previous Priorities

  • Reflect on the previous day's priorities.
  • Mark completed tasks as closed to acknowledge your progress.
  • Analyse any unfinished tasks and determine the next steps.

2. Manage Pending Tasks

  • Review tasks that are pending or waiting on others.
  • Decide to close, follow up, or adjust deadlines as necessary.
  • Keep your workflow streamlined and proactive.

3. Select New Priorities

  • Choose new priorities for the day (three main goals are suggested) from your open tasks.
  • Utilise the drag-and-drop feature to organise your tasks easily.
  • Open tasks are sorted by the last touched date, keeping your list up-to-date.

By following these steps, you'll ensure that your most important tasks are always front and centre. "Three Daily Goals" is more than just a task manager—it's a daily commitment to focused progress and personal growth. Download the app now and transform your daily routine into a pathway to success.
