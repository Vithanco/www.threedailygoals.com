How it works

  • Three goals a day. Each day, choose three meaningful tasks — your daily wins.
  • Compass Check. A short daily reflection that helps you pick your next goals and keep your direction.
  • No more clutter. Tasks you ignore for too long are moved to the graveyard — out of sight, out of guilt.
  • A streak that feels good. Every day you do a Compass Check, your streak grows — a small reward for staying present.

    Why it works

    Most tools treat tasks like a bucket you must empty. Real life is a river — it keeps flowing.

    Three Daily Goals embraces the river:

    • You pick three meaningful wins for the next day/today.
    • Stale items flow out to the graveyard after 30 days (you can revive anytime).
    • You keep momentum without the guilt of an ever-growing bucket.

    This reframes your to-dos from “finish everything” to “choose wisely and keep moving.”
