Frequently asked Questions

Question: Where does Three Daily Goals save its information?

Answer: The data is stored in your personal iCloud account to ensure it's available across all your devices. It is also cached locally on your devices using Apple's iCloud synchronisation and local storage mechanisms, ensuring quick access and updates.

Question: Who can access my information?

Answer: Your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account, making it accessible only to you under normal circumstances. An administrator with access to your device or iCloud account might also access the data. Rest assured, the software author has no access to your data without obtaining explicit consent from you. This process would involve clear communication and authorisation before any access is granted.
